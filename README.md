# SuperFrogScheduler 
### Timeline: Mar 2025 - May 2025

SuperFrog is TCU’s beloved Horned Frog Mascot. He attends all sporting events. There are an additional number of events (about 250 each year) around the TCU community that SuperFrog is expected to attend. SuperFrog does everything from reading at elementary schools, to visiting groups in the community, to weddings, to charity events, to golf tournaments. TCU Spirit Team has a team of about 8 students playing as the SuperFrog.

### Summary of events:
- Sports games (TCU football, basketball, baseball games)
- Campus events (Frogs First, Bid Day, etc.)
- Media shoots (Commercials, TV appearances, Photo shoots)
- Community appearances (Weddings, Birthdays, Grad Parties, Charity)

The goal of this project is to streamline the entire booking process and provide a better user experience for both customers and the TCU Spirit Team. The client, the TCU Spirit Team, would also like to remove most of the calls, emails, paper, and Google Docs involved, streamline the booking process.

### Product Features:
- User Account Management
- SuperFrog Team Management
- Reports
- Payment/Email
- Authentication
- Authorization
